Aral For All


Advocate for the accessibility of quality education for all.


5.5

Million
Many children who suffer from dyslexia, dyscalculia, dysgraphia, ADHD and Attention Deficit Disorder or ADD in the Philippines struggle to find quality education that can accommodate their unique needs. As a matter of fact, a study conducted by child rights organization “Save the Children Philippines” stated that 5.5 million Filipino learners with disabilities “already have limited access to quality and inclusive education” even before the pandemic.

No Budget for SPED

in National Expenditure Program
The proposed budget of DEPED for special education for 2023 was not considered in the Natural Expenditure Program. Special Education and children with disabilities are still not being made a priority in the governments programs. When it comes to actually putting in funding, they can't seem to do it.
